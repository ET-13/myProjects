I'm a pianist and composer, its actually what I went to school for.

I created this as a proof of concept/exercise in making an interactive webpage where kids and beginners could learn
piano and simple theory concepts using their own computer keyboard as a piano and reference. 

The Javascript, CSS, and HTML are all included in the "pianochordidentifier.html" file, the only dependencies are the mp3's and jpg
included in this folder. If you were to download it and open the html in your browser it should work without any issue. 
It works best on FireFox.
